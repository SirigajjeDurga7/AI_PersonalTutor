# 🎓 AI Personal Tutor Platform

An advanced AI-powered Learning Management System (LMS) designed to provide personalized learning experiences for students, instructors, and administrators. The platform combines course management, intelligent tutoring, quiz assessment, and progress tracking into a unified educational environment.

---

# 📖 Overview

AI Personal Tutor Platform enables students to enroll in courses, access learning materials, interact with an AI-powered tutor, attempt quizzes, and monitor their learning progress. Instructors can manage courses and educational resources, while administrators oversee platform operations. The integration of Artificial Intelligence provides instant academic assistance, personalized learning support, and enhanced student engagement.

# 🔄 Workflow

1. User registers and logs into the platform.
2. Student browses available courses.
3. Student enrolls in preferred courses.
4. Learning materials and notes are accessed.
5. Student interacts with the AI Tutor for doubt clarification.
6. AI Tutor generates intelligent responses.
7. Student attempts quizzes and assessments.
8. Quiz scores are evaluated and recorded.
9. Progress is tracked and displayed on the dashboard.
10. Instructors manage courses and learning resources.
11. Administrators monitor overall platform activities.
